I have developed a Fast-Search Inventory Tool designed for retail stores to optimize the shopping experience. Currently, many shops lack a centralized system that allows customers to quickly locate items, often leading to a disconnect between what is shown in the window display and what is actually available in stock.

By implementing this tool, stores can significantly reduce the stress on sales associates and eliminate the frustration of manual inventory checks. When customers can find what they need without friction, they are far more likely to become loyal, returning shoppers
